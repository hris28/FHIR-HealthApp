Project Activities

These activities were completed incrementally to explore different aspects of building applications on top of the FHIR API.

  • Activity 1: Patient CRUD
    Reading, creating, editing, and deleting patient data
  • Activity 2: Clinical Records
    Conditions, medications, and related resources
  • Activity 3: Highcharts
    Quantitative visualization of clinical observations
  • Activity 4: Leaflet Mapping
    Geographic visualization of healthcare locations
